We have some histroy behind us!
winning 4 League cups, 2 UCLS, 2 European Cups, One UEFA Cup 2 FA cups, 1 FA Charity Shield. Recently Forest were promoted to the Premier League beating Sheffield UTD on penaltys and Huddersfeild 1-0 Ryan Yates scoring the opening and winning goal
